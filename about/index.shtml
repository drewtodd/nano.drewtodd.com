About This Site

This site represents the first effort of a novice writer to produce a crappy novel (or, if things go really well, a crappy first draft) in a seemingly ridiculous timeframe. I’m using NaNoWriMo 2017 as the impetus to final get off my arse and write something substantial.

About the story

This story chronicles the events and adventures of Fodder Von Gilder — musician, half-breed, outcast — as he does his best to get by in Vanderhelm, the kingdom of men. He does some stuff, gets into some scrapes, presumably gets out of them. Or not. At this point I have no idea any firmer than that. This promises to be the literary equivalent of very young cheese: It’s runny and kind of oozes around. It stinks. But it will still somehow (hopefully) be enjoyable.

Check back at the end of November 2017 and I’ll have (also hopefully) written a more acceptable synopsis.

About the author

Andrew Todd (me) is a forty-something desk jockey, musician, blues nerd, and hack writer from Southern California. This story represents my first attempt at a novel and my first shot at NanoWriMo. If you’re also participating in this madness, look me up (my username is drewtodd) and add me as a writing buddy. I’m good at commiserating. When I’m not writing I’m likely picking a guitar, hanging out with my lovely wife, my Scottish Terrier, and one homicidal cat.
